❓ Frequently Asked Questions

💡 What is Onecoin?

Onecoin (ONE) is a limited-supply cryptocurrency built on a secure blockchain forked from Bitcoin. It uses Proof-of-Work (PoW) to ensure decentralization and fairness.

📦 How many Onecoin (ONE) are there?

The total supply is strictly limited to 20,000,000 ONE tokens: 15M for mining, 2M for the founder, 2M for development, and 1M held as a reserve.

🔐 Is the platform secure?

Yes. Onecoin is built with open-source, auditable code. All transactions are recorded on-chain. Your purchases are stored securely and transparently.

💵 How much does ONE cost?

ONE is sold at a fixed price of $10 USDT per token during the initial offering. Price will be determined by the market once listed publicly.

🌐 How can I buy ONE?

You can buy directly from the official platform using USDT (ERC20 or TRC20). Simply enter your wallet address and desired amount on the Buy ONE page.

📈 What is the future of Onecoin?

Onecoin aims to become a widely recognized digital asset with listings on major exchanges, real-world partnerships, and adoption in decentralized finance (DeFi) ecosystems.

📊 Why should I trust Onecoin?

There are no hidden ICOs, no anonymous teams. The distribution is public. The founder allocation is limited to 10%, and all investor funds are visible and tracked on-chain.

🧠 Why was this project created?

Onecoin was designed to give people a new opportunity—fairly, transparently, and early. It’s built for those who missed Bitcoin’s early days and believe in long-term blockchain utility.

🌍 Is it available internationally?

Yes! Onecoin is borderless and decentralized. Anyone with internet access and a crypto wallet can participate, regardless of country or currency.
